Develop your skills, learn something new and grow

Start.ng offers the best on hands practice in the world of programming. The training is for complete beginners who want to learn the basics of software development.

Project Details

The program is divided into 3 phases

  • Phase 1: Training, Duration: 6 weeks
  • Phase 2: Project, Duration: 2 weeks
  • Phase 3: Pre-HNG Internship, 1 week

You will receive more information about each phase when the program begins.
